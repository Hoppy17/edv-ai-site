About EDV AI Solutions.

The founder

Built on systems that actually matter.

My name is Glen Hopwood. I spent 10 years in the Australian Defence Force as a medic and in operational planning. After leaving I studied finance, which pulled me toward business systems and eventually into technology and automation, the same problem-solving mindset, different tools.


I'm based on the Mid North Coast, raising a family and running businesses in regional Australia. I know what it's like to juggle, to mean to get around to something and never quite find the time. AI is a tool, and like any tool it works best when it's set up properly for the job. I'm here to do that, fit it to your business, show you how it works, and hand it over.
